# Question 20: What are preprocessor directives?

**What are preprocessor directives?**
    - **Answer/Explanation:** Preprocessor directives are instructions to the compiler before compilation, like #include, #define.
    - **Code Example:**
      ```cpp
      #include <iostream>
      #define PI 3.14159
      ```
    - **Key Concepts:** Compilation process.
    - **Problem-Solving Technique:** Header inclusion, macros.
