# Question 38: What are vectors in C++?

**What are vectors in C++?**
    - **Answer/Explanation:** Vectors are dynamic arrays that can resize automatically.
    - **Code Example:**
      ```cpp
      std::vector<int> v;
      v.push_back(1);
      v.push_back(2);
      ```
    - **Key Concepts:** Dynamic sizing, random access.
    - **Problem-Solving Technique:** Array-like operations.
