# Question 19: Explain the ternary operator.

**Explain the ternary operator.**
    - **Answer/Explanation:** The ternary operator `? :` is a shorthand for if-else.
    - **Code Example:**
      ```cpp
      int max = (a > b) ? a : b;
      ```
    - **Key Concepts:** Conditional expressions.
    - **Problem-Solving Technique:** Concise conditionals.
