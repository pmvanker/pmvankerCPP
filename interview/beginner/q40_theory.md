# Question 40: What are sets in C++?

**What are sets in C++?**
    - **Answer/Explanation:** Sets are containers that store unique elements in sorted order.
    - **Code Example:**
      ```cpp
      #include <set>
      std::set<int> s = {1, 2, 3};
      ```
    - **Key Concepts:** Uniqueness, ordering.
    - **Problem-Solving Technique:** Unique element storage.
